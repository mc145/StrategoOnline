How to play Stratego

Pieces in Stratego

Stratego is a war based board game where players move their pieces to attack their opponent's pieces and capture their flag. To set up the game, both players place their pieces on their side of the board. Both players have a 10x4 grid to put their 40 pieces. The main point of the game is to put your flag in a strategic position so the enemy can not capture it. You also have bombs that are very powerful and can blow up anything that come across it except for the miner as it will mine out the bomb.
The pieces below are the pieces that are used in Stratego.
  • Spy: The spy is the weakest piece in the game, yet it can kill number ten, the marshal.
  • Marshal: The marshal is the most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack anything that it comes across
