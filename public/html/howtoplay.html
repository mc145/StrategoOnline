How to play Stratego

Pieces in Stratego

Stratego is a war based board game where players move their pieces to attack their opponent's pieces and capture their flag. To set up the game, both players place their pieces on their side of the board. Both players have a 10x4 grid to put their 40 pieces. The main point of the game is to put your flag in a strategic position so the enemy can not capture it. You also have bombs that are very powerful and can blow up anything that come across it except for the miner as it will mine out the bomb. The most powerful piece in the game is the marshal and it can take anything piece except for the bombs. All the main pieces are numbered, and all of the numbered pieces have a purpose. 10 is the Marshal, 9 is the General, 8 is the Colonel, 7 is the Major, If a numbered piece attacks the opponents piece and if the opponent's pieces' number is lower,
The pieces below are the pieces that are used in Stratego.
  • Spy: The spy is the weakest piece in the game, yet it can kill number ten, the marshal.
  • Marshal: The marshal is the most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack anything that it comes across.
  • General: The general is the second most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a numebr lower than 9.
  • Colonel: The colonel is the third most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a number lower than 8.
  • Major: The major is the fourth most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a number lower than 7.
  • Captain: The captain is the fifth most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a numbner lower than 6.
  • Lieutenant: The lieutenant is the sixth most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a number lower than 5.
  • Sergeant: The sergeant is the seventh most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a number lower than 4.
  • Miner: The miner is the eighth most powerful piece in the game, and it can attack any piece that has a number lower than 3. But the miner is the only piece that can attack and remove bombs from the opponent
  • Scout: The scout is the the ninth most powerful piece in the game, as it can attack any piece that has a number of 9, but it will end as a trade. But the scout is the only piece that can move however many places right, left, front, or back as long as nothing is stopping it from doing so.
  • Bomb: The bomb is a powerful tool that you can place around your side of the board to protect your flag and defend your pieces. Be aware though, the miner is the piece that can dig out these bombs.
  • Flag: The flag is the piece that you must protect at all costs. If the opponent moves any piece onto the flag, it's game over. Be wise about its position.
